## Overview

Visually impaired individuals struggle to understand their surroundings in real time. We made this project to find a way to help visually impaired individuals to sense their surroundings using sensors that sends signal to a motor. Since sensors are sometimes innaccurate, we decided to implement an AI Based Camera which can further improve the accuracy for the overall structure.
